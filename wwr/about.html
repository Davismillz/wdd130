Downstream Rafting

At Downstream Rafting, we believe adventure should be fearless, unforgettable, and safe. Since our founding, we've guided thousands of paddlers through some of the region's most thrilling rapids, pairing expert instruction with a genuine love for the water. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned rafter, our mission is simple: give you the ride of your life while getting you home with a story to tell.

History

Downstream Rafting started in 2010 as a two-boat operation run by a small group of river guides who couldn't imagine doing anything else for a living. What began as weekend trips for friends quickly grew into a full-fledged outfitter, known for its experienced crew and unmatched safety record. Today, we run daily expeditions down some of the country's most exhilarating rivers, but we've never lost that original, close-knit passion for the water that started it all.
