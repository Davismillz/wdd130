Nigeria

"Nigeria is a West African country of over 220 million people, making it the most populous nation in Africa and one of the most populous in the world. Its capital is Abuja, while Lagos is its largest city and commercial hub. Nigeria is known for its remarkable diversity, with over 250 ethnic groups (the largest being Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo) and more than 500 languages spoken, alongside English as the official language. The country is Africa's largest economy, driven significantly by oil and gas exports, though it also has a growing tech and entertainment sector—Nollywood is one of the world's largest film industries, and Afrobeats has become a major global music genre. Nigeria gained independence from British colonial rule in 1960 and is now a federal republic made up of 36 states plus the Federal Capital Territory."
